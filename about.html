ABOUT Welcome to our website dedicated to the multifaceted artist Joji! Formerly known as YouTube personality Filthy Frank, Joji has since gained a devoted following with his musical talent, encompassing a range of genres from lo-fi hip-hop to R&B. Born George Miller, he's a Japanese-Australian artist who has made a name for himself with his unique sound, honest lyrics, and captivating performances. On this website, you'll find the latest news and updates about Joji's music career, from his albums and singles to his live shows and collaborations. We'll delve into his backstory and influences, exploring the creative journey that led him to become the artist he is today. You'll also find exclusive interviews and insights from Joji himself, as well as from those who have worked with him. Whether you're a long-time fan or just discovering Joji's music, we hope our website will provide a comprehensive and informative resource for all things Joji. Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to sharing more about this talented artist with you!
